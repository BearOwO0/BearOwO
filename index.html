This is Yoasobi

Yoasobi is a Japanese music superduo formed by Sony Music Entertainment Japan, composed of Vocaloid producer Ayase, and singer-songwriter Ikura.

Origin -- Tokyo, Japan

Genres -- J-pop

Years active -- 2019–present
Represented by the slogan "novel into music", the duo has released songs based on novels posted on Monogatary.com , a novel-centered social media operated by their label, which is also from various media like novels written by professional authors, books, and letters.
